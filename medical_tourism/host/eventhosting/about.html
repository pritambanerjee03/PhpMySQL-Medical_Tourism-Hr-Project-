About Hastings Hanumant

Hastings hanumant welfare society is an NGO in India directly benefitting over 1000 women,youth and children in India, through health check up, eye check up camp, women empowerment programme, old age home, free computer classes, free drawing classes, free beautician classes ,free dish TV courses, freejudo karate classes, free dance classes and hunting talents from slums. This NGO has 500 volunteers and has 2 centres in Kolkata.
